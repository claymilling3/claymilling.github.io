MY PASSION FOR MUSIC

From a a young age, I was listening to my Dad play the guitar. It was then when I realized that I wanted to play music. I started by playing the guitar, which I enjoyed, but I always wanted to play the drums. I finally got the opportunity to go over to one of my dads friend's house who was a professional drummer and I was able to play his kit. From then on, I was hooked. I imediatly started taking lessons after that and I haven't stoped playing since.

MY FAVORITE BANDS

I have many favorite bands, as I enjoy going to concerts and seeing a variety of music. Moe. is one of my favorite bands, as I have seen them several times live and they never fail to impress. I think they are great musicians and I really enjoy the type of music they play. The first time I ever saw them, I was young and as I have grown older I continue to see them as much as possible. They are coming to the Georgia Theatre soona and I can't wait to see them again!

FUTURE GOALS

I want to continue playing drums for the rest of my life. I am currently playing in a band and I hope to start playing in front of larger and larger crowds. Even if drumming becomes just a hobby rather than a profession, I want to stay playing in local bands around where I live and play events that give back to the community. I also want to play a show with my brother at some point. He is a bass player and it would be awesome if we could play together at some point, which I'm sure we will be able to do.

Rock and roll music, if you like it, if you feel it, you can't help but move to it. That's what happens to me. I can't help it.
