  • LIVE DOWNLOADS FEATURED SHOW

    10/31/15 ~ The Fillmore ~ Philadelphia, PA

    Need some traveling tunes to Philly this weekend, well we got you covered by taking you back to “a long time ago in a galaxy far, far away”. Well maybe not that far being only a year, but last year’s Star Wars Halloween at the Fillmore was one of the biggest shows to date and if you haven’t already this is without question a “do yourself a favor” download. This show emcompassed 3 varieties of songs that encapsulated the theme perfectly, with moe.’s take on John Williams’ directing the London Symphony Orchestra in his written masterpieces that are some of the most recognizable composed pieces of music in the world, altered Star Wars themed lyrics and puns on moe. originals and new, and debuted covers. Anyone that was at this show will agree if you call moe.’s Halloween Star Wars rock fest was the force, “force is light, light is good, force is good”! Now get ready for this year’s double feature TARANTINOE. HALLOWEENOE.
